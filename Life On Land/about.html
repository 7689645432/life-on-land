LIFE ON LAND

Background

Microbial mats may have been the first organisms to colonise dry land

Life On Land pertains to the efforts and actions taken to preserve, protect and restore terrestorial eco systems and bio diversity.It involves land management practices, re afforestation, afforestation, conservation of natural habitats and combating desertification and land degredation.

Purpose

A flourishing life on land on land is the foundation for our life on this planet's ecosystem and we have caused severe damage to it through deforestation, loss of natural habitats and land degredation. promoting a sustainable use of our ecosystems and preserving biodiversity is not a cause. it is the key to our own survival.

Look after land and it will look after you, destroy it and it will destroy you.
